Welcome to Daphnes!

About Feudal lords hear my plea! No longer will your surfs control you. With Daphnes, untold power and control will be yours! Daphnes is a modern solution to managing rental properties. Track repairs, warrenties, rents, and more! Need a new lease or application? Helpful reminds to pay rents on time? Not a problem. Daphnes is completely open source and totally free too!

Features

Modern Design

Daphnes utilizes modern, mobile first design paterns that keep the clutter out of your way so you can get back to work.

Purpose Driven

Built by landlords, for landlords. Every inch of this platform is built around getting things done and getting back to life.

Integration

Craigslist, Postlets, Airbnb, Flickr and more. Daphines doesn't pick favorites and neither should you! Use the best tool for your business.
